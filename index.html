Unleashed WooCommerce integration connects your inventory management system directly with your online store. Instead of manually updating stock levels, processing orders separately, or reconciling data at the end of the day, the integration automatically syncs critical business information between platforms.

When implemented through SKUPlugs, the integration allows:

  • Real-time inventory synchronization
  • Automated order and product data transfer
